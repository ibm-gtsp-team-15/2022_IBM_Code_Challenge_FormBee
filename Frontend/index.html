About


FormBee is a platform that is centered around a chatbot powered by Rasa, helping you to take government forms and surveys at your leisure and manage them. It is a one-stop shop form and survey filling platform that manages all your forms in a centralised repository. It also has functions for exporting pdf and mailing to authorities.



Features


A chatbot powered by the Rasa framework listens to your input to fill in forms and surveys. The bot validates your input automatically and reports if there is anything wrong.

Clearing queries regarding the form can be delegated to the bot. It will peruse its database and the internet if required.
